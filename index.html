My First Week At AkiraChix

My first week at AkiraChix was delightful and full of activities. At first it was quite difficult to catch up with
the new routine but later on I adapted and started enjoying the program. The classes are a bit intense yet
very educative. The trainers are doing their best in teaching us and also providing us with the necessary
educative materials. This is an opportunity for me to be open minded and explore every aspect of tech.

The Good Experiences

  2. The rooms are tidy and comfortable.
  3. I enjoyed learning new things

The Bad Experiences
